# Vrinda-Store-Data-Analysis-Interactive-Excel-Sales-Dashboard
1) Women drive the majority of sales (~65%) 
2) Adult age group contributes the highest number of orders, especially among women
3) Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the top-performing states (~35%)
4) Amazon,Flipkart and Myntra dominate as sales channels (~80%)
5) Sales peak in the early months (Jan–Mar) and gradually decline towards year-end, suggesting seasonal demand patterns
Recommendation to improve Vrinda store details-
1) Target women customers of age group (30-49 yrs) living in Maharashtra, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh by showing ads/offers/coupons available on Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra
2) Run seasonal offers in low-sales month
